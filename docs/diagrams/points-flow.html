Partners Points — How Points Flow

Customers earn points when they pay in store, then choose what to do with them. Follow the arrows.

In store Customer app Our engine Lulu
1 In store

Enters mobile number

At the payment terminal the customer types their mobile number, then pays. New numbers are signed up automatically.

2 Our engine

Points are added

Partners Points adds points based on how much they spent.

3 Customer app

Balance updates

Their points balance goes up in the app straight away.

4 Customer

Customer decides

They choose what to do with their points — one of the two below.

↓  The customer picks ONE  ↓

Option A Use points like cash, in the same shop

5 In store

Pay with points

At the terminal they choose to spend points instead of cash.

6 In store

Comes off the bill

The points are taken off the total like money. Done.

Option B Turn points into Lulu Happiness Points

5 Customer app

Convert to Lulu

They send their points to their linked Lulu account.

6 Lulu

Happiness Points arrive

The matching Lulu points land in their Lulu account.

Who pays for it? The shop. Each conversion is paid from the shop's prepaid Lulu wallet — money they top up in advance. If it runs low they're reminded; if it runs out, converting pauses until they top up again.

Customers always keep earning points. Converting to Lulu only works while the shop's prepaid wallet has funds.
